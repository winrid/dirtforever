How to control Dirt Rally

1. Start Executable
2. Wait 15 seconds
3. Hit Enter ("hit enter to start" screen)

On Main Menu

Target=Events
Sequence:Enter

=========

Target: Clubs
Sequence:

1. F4
2. Right Arrow
3. Right Arrow
4. Down Arrow
5. Enter

Should load clubs page.

=========

Target: Free Play
Sequence:

1. F4
2. Right Arrow
3. Right Arrow
4. Enter
5. Enter (select map, enter selects default)
6. Down Arrow
7. Down Arrow
8. Enter
9. Enter (selects car, starts loading map)
10. Wait 30s
11. Hit Enter

From here you can drive car and then leave.
